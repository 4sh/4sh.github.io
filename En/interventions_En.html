Technologies

Choosing the right technology for your development is a key decision which is influenced by multiple factors and constraints: Technical requirements, historical development, internal resources, strategy and value creation, technological edge.

Our area of competence, structured through two main platforms (Java and .Net), and our expertise are the building blocks of our product offering: we recommend superior tailored solutions. We help our clients to choose the right product architecture by a careful analysis of potential scenarios.

Our team will support your efforts to implement the adequate recommendations regarding :

  • Architecture norms (SOA, EDA, REST, MDA, ...)
  • User interface (rich user, light user, RIA, smart client, AJAX, ...)
  • Module (open source, CRM, ...)
  • Methodology (flexible, scrum, lean, TDD, BDD, ...)
  • Tools (Trouble and issue tracking, set up, continuous integration, built system, IDE, …)
  • Cloud computing (virtualization, Amazon S3, EC2, ...)
  • Development 
    • User Interface (ISF, Struts, Wicket, Grails, Seam, Adobe Flex, Eclipse RCP, ...)
    • Server ((Spring, EJB3, Mule ESB, ServiceMix, Jackrabbit, JBossAS, Glassfish, ...) 
    • Data (JPA, Hibernate, JCR, MySQL, Oracle, ...)
