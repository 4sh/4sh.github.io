SYNTYS Groupe

4SH means “4th Software Houses”, which is an analogy to the 4PL norm used broadly in logistics and supply chain management. 4SH is the matrix and driver of Syntys’ externalization strategy for the IT Services.

SYNTYS is a specialized industrial group which helps companies to externalize specific non-core functions. Legal services, IT Infrastructure, Supply Chain management, Marketing and Procurement could be optimized through superior integration and outsourcing. Companies haven’t built the industrial competences to manage this complex strategic process.

SYNTYS pledges expanding and pushing further the limits of externalization and outsourcing and give its clients tools to take up new exciting challenges.

SYNTYS has adopted honesty, sincerity and humility as the building blocks of its corporate culture. These core values reinforce the group’s consistency and strength.
